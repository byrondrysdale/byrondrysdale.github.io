Player of the Season

Diego Costa

Diego Costa of Chelsea has been the player of the season thus far. He has started the premier league with 12 goals in just 15 games. He continues to prove that he should be considered one of the best strikers in the league and in the world. Game in and game out he is the go to guy for Antonio Conte, the manager of Chelsea Football Club. The way Chelsea and Costa are playing, they are real contenders for the title

Title Predictions

Arsenal Football Club Title Winners?

Arsenal started the premier league with a loss against Liverpool. Dissapointing result for the Gunners but they have since cleaned up their act, disposing of Chelsea 3-0, and they are currently in 2nd place. Can Arsenal continue their good run of form and top the table to win the Premier League? Every season they come 4th...However, with Ozil and Sanchez in the form of their lives,this could be their year.
