Diego Costa of Chelsea has started the premier league with 12 goals in just 15 games. He continues to prove that he should be considered one of the best strikers in the league and in the world. Game in and game out he is the go to guy for Antonio Conte, the manager of Chelsea Football Club. The way Chelsea and Costa are playing, they are real contenders for the title
